About Us

Shivaji Polytechnic College, Sangola is a premier self-financed engineering diploma institution located in Solapur, Maharashtra. Established in 2009 by RTES, the college is approved by AICTE and affiliated with MSBTE, Mumbai.


The college offers Diploma programs in Computer Engineering, Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering and provides excellent facilities, laboratories, library, hostel and placement support.
